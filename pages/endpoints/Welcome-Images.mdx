## Welcome Image Templates

**Example Request:**
```url filename="URL"
https://api.some-random-api.com/welcome/img/:template/:background?type=join&textcolor=green&username=test&guildName=test&memberCount=5&avatar=https://img.pyrocdn.com/dbKUgahg.png
```

<Callout type="warning">
    Note: The <Code>:template</Code> and <Code>:background</Code> need to be replaced with the template and background you want to use.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
    Note: Some templates will not display every single query parameter even though they are all required.
</Callout>
